### Data Scientist Quality Dimensions

When validating data science requirements and specifications, ensure these quality dimensions are addressed:

#### Problem Definition & Research Question

**Problem Statement**:
- Is the business problem clearly articulated?
- Are success criteria quantified and measurable?
- Is the problem type specified (classification, regression, clustering, ranking)?
- Are constraints and limitations documented?

**Research Questions**:
- Are research questions clearly formulated?
- Is hypothesis documented?
- Are assumptions explicitly stated?
- Are alternative hypotheses considered?

**Success Metrics**:
- Are business success metrics defined?
- Are technical success metrics specified?
- Is measurement strategy documented?
- Are baseline performance metrics established?

#### Data Exploration & Understanding

**Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**:
- Are EDA requirements specified?
- Is data profiling strategy documented?
- Are visualization requirements defined?
- Is statistical summary analysis strategy specified?

**Data Quality Assessment**:
- Are data quality dimensions defined (completeness, accuracy, consistency)?
- Is missing data analysis strategy specified?
- Are outlier detection requirements documented?
- Is data validation strategy defined?

**Data Distribution Analysis**:
- Is distribution analysis strategy specified?
- Are normality test requirements documented?
- Is skewness and kurtosis analysis strategy defined?
- Are correlation analysis requirements specified?

**Feature Distribution**:
- Is feature distribution analysis strategy specified?
- Are univariate analysis requirements documented?
- Is bivariate analysis strategy defined?
- Are multivariate analysis requirements specified?

#### Data Preparation & Feature Engineering

**Data Cleaning**:
- Is missing data handling strategy specified (imputation, deletion)?
- Are outlier treatment requirements documented?
- Is data normalization/standardization strategy defined?
- Are data type conversion requirements specified?

**Feature Engineering**:
- Are feature creation strategies documented?
- Is feature extraction method specified?
- Are feature transformation requirements defined?
- Is feature interaction strategy documented?

**Feature Selection**:
- Is feature selection method specified (filter, wrapper, embedded)?
- Are feature importance analysis requirements documented?
- Is dimensionality reduction strategy defined?
- Are feature correlation analysis requirements specified?

**Data Transformation**:
- Are transformation techniques specified (log, square root, box-cox)?
- Is encoding strategy documented (one-hot, label, target)?
- Are scaling requirements defined?
- Is binning strategy specified?

#### Statistical Analysis

**Descriptive Statistics**:
- Are descriptive statistics requirements specified?
- Is central tendency analysis strategy documented?
- Are dispersion measures requirements defined?
- Is distribution shape analysis strategy specified?

**Inferential Statistics**:
- Are hypothesis testing requirements specified?
- Is statistical significance level defined (alpha)?
- Are confidence interval requirements documented?
- Is p-value interpretation strategy specified?

**Statistical Tests**:
- Are appropriate statistical tests identified?
- Is test selection rationale documented?
- Are test assumptions validated?
- Are alternative tests considered?

**Correlation & Causation**:
- Is correlation analysis strategy specified?
- Are causation analysis requirements documented?
- Is confounding variable identification strategy defined?
- Are causal inference requirements specified?

#### Modeling Strategy

**Model Selection**:
- Are candidate models identified and justified?
- Is model complexity vs. interpretability trade-off documented?
- Are ensemble methods considered?
- Is baseline model strategy specified?

**Model Training**:
- Is training strategy documented?
- Are hyperparameter tuning requirements specified?
- Is cross-validation strategy defined?
- Are training stopping criteria documented?

**Model Comparison**:
- Are model comparison metrics specified?
- Is model selection criteria documented?
- Are statistical comparison tests defined?
- Is model interpretability comparison strategy specified?

**Ensemble Methods**:
- Are ensemble strategies considered (bagging, boosting, stacking)?
- Is ensemble weight determination method specified?
- Are diversity measures for ensemble members defined?
- Is ensemble performance validation strategy documented?

#### Model Evaluation & Validation

**Evaluation Metrics**:
- Are evaluation metrics appropriate for problem type?
- Are metrics priorities specified?
- Is metric selection rationale documented?
- Are custom metrics defined if needed?

**Validation Strategy**:
- Is validation approach specified (hold-out, k-fold, stratified)?
- Are validation data requirements documented?
- Is temporal validation strategy defined (for time-series)?
- Are nested cross-validation requirements specified?

**Error Analysis**:
- Is error analysis strategy specified?
- Are error pattern identification requirements documented?
- Is residual analysis strategy defined?
- Are misclassification analysis requirements specified?

**Model Diagnostics**:
- Are diagnostic tests specified (residual plots, Q-Q plots)?
- Is overfitting/underfitting detection strategy documented?
- Are learning curve analysis requirements defined?
- Is model stability analysis strategy specified?

#### Uncertainty Quantification

**Confidence Intervals**:
- Are confidence interval requirements specified?
- Is confidence level defined?
- Is interval estimation method documented?
- Are bootstrap/resampling requirements specified?

**Prediction Intervals**:
- Are prediction interval requirements specified?
- Is uncertainty propagation strategy documented?
- Are probabilistic prediction requirements defined?
- Is Bayesian approach consideration documented?

**Sensitivity Analysis**:
- Is sensitivity analysis strategy specified?
- Are perturbation analysis requirements documented?
- Is what-if analysis strategy defined?
- Are robustness testing requirements specified?

#### Reproducibility & Documentation

**Reproducibility Requirements**:
- Is random seed management strategy specified?
- Are environment specifications documented (Python version, packages)?
- Is code versioning strategy defined?
- Are data versioning requirements specified?

**Analysis Documentation**:
- Is analysis workflow documented?
- Are decisions and rationale captured?
- Is negative result documentation strategy defined?
- Are assumptions and limitations documented?

**Notebook Organization**:
- Is notebook structure standard defined?
- Are markdown cell requirements specified?
- Is code cell organization strategy documented?
- Is output cell management strategy defined?

#### Data Visualization

**Exploratory Visualization**:
- Are exploratory visualization requirements specified?
- Is plot type selection strategy documented?
- Are interactive visualization requirements defined?
- Is dashboard creation strategy specified?

**Explanatory Visualization**:
- Are explanatory visualization requirements specified?
- Is visualization clarity criteria defined?
- Are color scheme requirements documented?
- Is accessibility consideration specified?

**Model Interpretation Visualization**:
- Are model interpretation visualization requirements specified?
- Is feature importance visualization strategy documented?
- Are partial dependence plot requirements defined?
- Is SHAP/LIME visualization strategy specified?

#### Experimentation & A/B Testing

**Experiment Design**:
- Is experiment design strategy specified?
- Are sample size requirements documented?
- Is randomization strategy defined?
- Are control group requirements specified?

**Statistical Power**:
- Is statistical power analysis requirements specified?
- Is minimum detectable effect size defined?
- Are Type I and Type II error rates documented?
- Is power calculation method specified?

**A/B Test Validation**:
- Is A/B test validation strategy specified?
- Are sample ratio mismatch detection requirements documented?
- Is novelty/primacy effect consideration defined?
- Is sequential testing strategy specified?

#### Domain Knowledge Integration

**Domain Expert Collaboration**:
- Is domain expert collaboration strategy documented?
- Are domain knowledge validation requirements specified?
- Is feature engineering domain input strategy defined?
- Is model result interpretation collaboration strategy documented?

**Domain Constraints**:
- Are domain-specific constraints documented?
- Is regulatory compliance consideration specified?
- Are business rules integration requirements defined?
- Is domain-specific validation strategy documented?

**Subject Matter Expertise**:
- Are SME input collection requirements specified?
- Is domain literature review strategy documented?
- Are industry benchmark comparison requirements defined?
- Is domain best practices integration strategy specified?

#### Ethical Considerations

**Bias Detection**:
- Is bias detection strategy specified?
- Are protected attributes identified?
- Is fairness metric selection documented?
- Are bias mitigation requirements defined?

**Ethical Review**:
- Is ethical review process documented?
- Are potential harms identified?
- Is benefit-risk analysis strategy defined?
- Are ethical approval requirements specified?

**Transparency**:
- Is model transparency requirement specified?
- Are decision explanation requirements documented?
- Is stakeholder communication strategy defined?
- Is public disclosure requirement specified?

#### Communication & Reporting

**Stakeholder Communication**:
- Is stakeholder communication strategy documented?
- Are technical vs. non-technical communication requirements specified?
- Is visualization for communication strategy defined?
- Are presentation requirements documented?

**Report Structure**:
- Is report structure standard defined?
- Are executive summary requirements specified?
- Is technical appendix strategy documented?
- Are recommendation formatting requirements defined?

**Insights & Recommendations**:
- Are insight extraction requirements specified?
- Is recommendation prioritization strategy documented?
- Is actionability criteria defined?
- Are implementation roadmap requirements specified?

---

**Quality Gate Checklist**: Before marking analysis as "ready for implementation":

- [ ] Problem definition, research questions, and success metrics are clearly specified
- [ ] Data exploration and understanding requirements include EDA, quality assessment, and distribution analysis
- [ ] Data preparation and feature engineering strategies are documented
- [ ] Statistical analysis requirements specify descriptive, inferential statistics, and appropriate tests
- [ ] Modeling strategy includes selection, training, comparison, and ensemble methods
- [ ] Model evaluation and validation specify metrics, validation approach, error analysis, and diagnostics
- [ ] Uncertainty quantification requirements include confidence intervals, prediction intervals, and sensitivity analysis
- [ ] Reproducibility and documentation standards are comprehensive
- [ ] Data visualization requirements cover exploratory, explanatory, and model interpretation visualizations
- [ ] Experimentation and A/B testing design is specified (if applicable)
- [ ] Domain knowledge integration strategy is documented
- [ ] Ethical considerations include bias detection, ethical review, and transparency requirements
- [ ] Communication and reporting requirements specify stakeholder strategy, structure, and insights
